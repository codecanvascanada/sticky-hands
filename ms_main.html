About The Game

A unique blend of merge puzzle and survival strategy. Craft, explore, and survive in a post-apocalyptic world. Every merge counts as you fight for your life against mutants and scavengers. Can you rebuild civilization from the remnants of the old world?

Features

Strategic Merging

Combine items to craft tools, weapons, and shelter.

Vast Wasteland

Explore a huge, procedurally generated world full of dangers and secrets.

Base Building

Design and build your own fortified camp to protect yourself from threats.
